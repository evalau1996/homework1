The Visualization of Air Quality

How many indexes do you know related to air quality?

And how much do you know about them?

  1. Air Quality Index(AQI)
  2. Air Quality Health Index(AQHI)
  3. Hedley Environmental Index

Air Quality Index(AQI)

AQI makes full use of people’s reaction towards colours.

  • Green represents safety and pureness.
  • Red symbolizes danger and warns people.
  • The darker the color is, the more serious the air quality will be.
  • Yellow and Orange are just between them .

Air Quality Health Index(AQHI)

AQHI is the Hong Kong version of AQI, released in 2013.

Just like AQI, different colours conveys different signals.

However, the drawbacks of AQI and AQHI are similar.

  • Does green represent absolute safety？
  • Can all the danger levels be simply divided into those colors?
  • For those who suffered long-term air pollution, can the AQI/AQHI still warn them?

Hedley Environmental Index

Released by HKU, Hedley Environment Index calculates the economic losses caused by air pollution.

Its official website updates the data instantaneously.
